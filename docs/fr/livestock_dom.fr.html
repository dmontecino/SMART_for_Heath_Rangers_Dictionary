Contexte

MLIST

The context in which the animals of the corresponding species are found. Multiple options can be selected

En liberté

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are observed in their habitat and not hunted, captured, captive, or restricted. These animals could be captured for health surveillance purposes and still be considered 'free-ranging'

Traqués et déplacés

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are hunted or captured at the time of the observation; however, they have been displaced from the original place of hunt or capture. For example, caged animals in posession of a walking hunter in a protected area

Chassé et non déplacé

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are hunted or captured at the time of the observation; however, they have not been displaced from the original place of hunt or capture. For example, animals observed in the trap set by a hunter

Centre de secours/réadaptation

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are kept in a rehabilitation/rescue center

Ferme

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are kept in a farm

Ménage

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are kept in a household

Recherche ou capture pour conservation

Animals of the corresponding species are registered as part of a research or conservation capture not related with health surveillance

Marché

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are kept in a market

Poste commercial

Animals of the corresponding species registered at the Incident are kept in a trade post
