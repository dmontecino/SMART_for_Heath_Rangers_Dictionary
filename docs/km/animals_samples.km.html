Captivity Category

LIST

The captivity category of the animal observed at the Incident

Free-ranging

The animal observed at the Incident is found in its habitat and not hunted, captured, captive, or restricted. The animal captured for health surveillance purposes is considered 'free-ranging'

Hunted And Displaced

The animal observed at the Incident is hunted or captured at the time of the observation; however, it has been displaced from the original place of hunt or capture. For example, a caged animal in posession of a walking hunter in a protected area

Hunted and Not Displaced

The animal observed at the Incident is hunted or captured at the time of the observation; however, it has not been displaced from the original place of hunt or capture. For example, an animal observed in the trap set by a hunter

Rescue/Rehabilitation Center

The animal observed at the Incident is kept in a rehabilitation/rescue center

Farm

The animal observed at the Incident is kept in a farm

Household

The animal observed at the Incident is kept in a household

Research or Conservation Capture

The animal observed at the Incident is captured as part of a research or conservation initiative not related with health surveillance

Market
